MessApp Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026 | Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Introduction

MessApp is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our encrypted messaging application.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email Address: Required for authentication and account recovery
  • Username: Display name within the app
  • Profile Picture: Optional avatar for identification
  • Bio/Status: Optional personal information

2. Content Data

  • Messages: End-to-end encrypted messages that we cannot read
  • Media Files: Images and files you share (encrypted)
  • Reactions: Emoji reactions to messages

3. Technical Data

  • Device Information: Operating system, device type
  • Usage Analytics: App performance metrics, crash reports
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure session management

4. Encryption Keys

  • Public Keys: Stored securely for message encryption
  • Private Keys: Encrypted and stored locally with your PIN
  • Backup Keys: Optional cloud backup (encrypted with PIN)

How We Use Your Information

1. Core Functionality

  • Message Delivery: Route encrypted messages between users
  • Authentication: Verify your identity and secure your account
  • Encryption: Manage public/private key pairs for E2EE

2. App Improvement

  • Crash Reports: Automatically report app crashes to improve stability
  • Performance Metrics: Monitor app performance and optimize experience
  • Usage Analytics: Understand feature usage to improve the app

3. Support

  • Customer Support: Respond to your questions and issues
  • Account Recovery: Help you recover your account if needed

Data Security

End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)

All messages are encrypted using AES-GCM-256. Keys are derived using ECDH P-256 curve. We cannot access your message content. Private keys are encrypted with your PIN.

Storage Security

  • Private keys stored locally with PBKDF2 encryption
  • Optional cloud backup encrypted with your PIN
  • Public keys stored securely for key exchange
  • Database access is restricted and monitored

Transmission Security

  • All communications use HTTPS/TLS
  • Real-time connections use secure WebSocket (WSS)
  • API endpoints require authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data We Don't Collect

We are committed to zero-access encryption. This means:

  • We cannot read your encrypted messages
  • We don't track you for advertising purposes
  • We don't collect location data
  • We don't access your contact lists
  • We don't store call logs or voice data
  • We don't collect financial information

Third-Party Services

Supabase (Database & Authentication)

  • Provides secure database and authentication services
  • Stores encrypted metadata and public keys
  • Processes authentication requests
  • Privacy Policy

Firebase (Push Notifications & Analytics)

  • Sends push notifications for new messages
  • Collects anonymous crash reports and performance data
  • Privacy Policy

Google Play Services (Android)

  • Required for push notifications and app functionality
  • Minimal data collection for essential services
  • Privacy Policy

Your Rights

1. Access and Portability

  • Download your account data
  • Export your messages (decrypted with your keys)
  • Transfer your data to other services

2. Correction and Deletion

  • Update your profile information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information

3. Control and Consent

  • Grant or revoke permissions
  • Opt-out of analytics (limited functionality)
  • Manage notification preferences

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until account deletion
  • Messages: Retained until you delete them
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 26 months
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 12 months
  • Backup Keys: Retained until account deletion
